Instructions


  • You need to guess the correct number.
  • The number does not repeat its digits.
  • The first number will contain 3 digits (level 1) and the last number will have 10 digits (level 7).
  • For every digit position you guess, you get +1.
  • If do not hit any position, you will get "no match".
  • For every digit you hit but it is not in the correct position, you will get -1.
  • You start with 3 lives and you have 10 attemps per live. If you lose them all, Game Over.
